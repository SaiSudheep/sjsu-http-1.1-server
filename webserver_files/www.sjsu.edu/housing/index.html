University Housing Services

University Housing Services strives to create a residential community that supports and enhances academic success, fosters the learning and development of our students, and promotes student involvement and civic engagement. Visit the new application website below. If you have visited the old portal previously, clear your internet browser's cache and restart before opening the link.

Access the Housing Application Portal

Get in Touch with the Team

Have any questions or need assistance? Contact University Housing Services between 8:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m. by emailing uhs-frontdesk@sjsu.edu or calling (408)795-5600. For in-person or after-hours assistance, housing staff are available at the front desks of each housing facility.

Email the Front Desk

Payment & Billing

Questions or comments regarding payments, installment payment plans, or billing? Email the Housing Cashier at uhs-cashier@sjsu.edu. or call (408) 795-5600.

Email the Housing Cashier

Housing Virtual Queue

Don't have time to wait on the phone to talk to someone? Join the Housing Virtual Queue. Let us know when you are available, whether you prefer a phone conversation or in-person visit, and someone from Housing will contact you during business hours.

Sign Up for a Call or Meeting

SJSU Immunization Requirements

All students living in on campus housing are required to be vaccinated against COVID-19 with recent and approved immunizations.  Refer to the links below for information about the housing and campus vaccine requirements and latest COVID-19 response updates.

  • Human Papillomavirus (HPV) Vaccination

Accommodations

University Housing Services works in collaboration with the Accessible Education Center (AEC) to provide housing accommodations for students with documented disabilities who meet the eligibility and verification requirements. Students requesting an accommodation should review the Housing Accommodations information prior to completing the Housing Application.

SJSU International House

Have you considered living in on-campus housing at the International House? The International House (I-House) is home to U.S. and international students who are freshman through graduate students.  As an SJSU housing option, the I-House community provides a diverse and welcoming intercultural living environment. Click here for updates about the I-House application process. 

Frosh Live On Requirement

The Frosh Live-On Requirement is suspended for the 2024-2025 academic year. No first-year student is required to live in campus housing or complete and submit an exemption form.

Residential Life

Living on campus places you in the center of the Spartan community and in the heart of Silicon Valley. Take advantage of the opportunities to meet other students, participate in residence community programs, and have easy access to student organizations, campus events and a variety of academic resources.
