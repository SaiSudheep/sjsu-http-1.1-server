The Student Wellness Center at SJSU

Welcome to the Student Wellness Center

The Student Wellness Center is home to your counseling, medical, and well-being needs at SJSU. Visit your doctor, talk to your counselor, relax in the Wellness Lounge, and more!

SJSU Survivor Advocacy Services

The SJSU Survivor Advocacy Services (SAS) offers a safe and supportive environment for people who have been impacted by sexual violence, intimate partner violence, domestic violence, and stalking. We are a confidential resource for SJSU students, faculty and staff members where you can discuss what happened without notifying the Title IX office or law enforcement.

Grow your Well-being

YOU@SJSU

YOU@SJSU is designed to help students Succeed, Thrive, and Matter by managing stress, settling goals, promoting happiness and well-being, and connecting to campus resources. 

eCHECKUP TO GO

eCHECKUP TO GO is a series of interactive online tools designed to give personalized feedback on your use of alcohol, cannabis, and/or tobacco.

Wellness Lounge

Take a break in our space, reserve a massage chair appointment, and pick up safer sex supplies. 

Well-being @ SJSU

Well-being @ SJSU is a hub of resources and experiences to inspire and empower you to grow your well-being.
